Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Overview

This privacy policy explains how apps published by Lukqo may handle information. Specific data practices can vary by app and platform.

Dio

Dio may use third-party services required for app functionality, analytics, advertising, crash reporting or content delivery. Those services may process device information, app activity, identifiers or diagnostic data according to their own privacy policies and the permissions granted by the user.

Dio does not intentionally request sensitive personal information unless it is necessary for a clearly disclosed app feature.

Advertising

Some apps may display advertising provided by third-party advertising partners. These partners may use device identifiers or related information to provide, measure and protect advertising services, subject to platform settings and applicable law.

Children

The apps are not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children where doing so would require parental consent under applicable law.

Changes

This policy may be updated when app features, services or legal requirements change. The latest version will be published on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the support contact listed on the official Google Play store page for the relevant app.
